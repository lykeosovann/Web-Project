Our History

KOI Thé is a popular bubble tea brand that originated in Taiwan as part of the 50 Lan chain before rebranding to expand internationally. Known for its premium quality teas and unique golden tapioca pearls, KOI Thé offers a refined tea experience with a focus on fresh ingredients and customizable sweetness and ice levels. The brand has grown globally, with locations across Asia, the U.S., and other regions. Its signature drinks, such as Golden Bubble Milk Tea, Oolong Milk Tea, and Green Tea Macchiato, have helped establish KOI Thé as a go-to destination for bubble tea lovers, providing a consistent and high-quality beverage experience worldwide.
